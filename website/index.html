Features

Everything a child needs, nothing they don't

KidPod locks down any Android phone so kids can enjoy music and audiobooks without getting distracted.

Kiosk Lockdown

Kids can't leave the app. Home button, recent apps, and status bar are all disabled in kid mode.

Music Playback

Plays MP3, FLAC, M4A, and OGG. Browse by artist, album, or all songs. Shuffle and repeat modes.

Audiobooks

Supports M4B and MP3 audiobooks. Auto-saves position every 30 seconds. Resume exactly where they left off.

Parent PIN

4–6 digit PIN lets parents access settings. Encrypted with AES-256 and SHA-256 hashing. 5-attempt lockout.

USB Content Transfer

Drag-and-drop files from any computer. Connect via USB, add files to /KidPod/Music/, done.

Volume Limit

Set a maximum volume to protect young ears. Kids physically cannot turn it up beyond the limit you set.

How It Works

Up and running in 15 minutes

No technical knowledge needed. Just an old Android phone and a few minutes of setup.

1

Install KidPod

Download the APK and install it on any Android 8.0+ phone. No Play Store required.

2

Activate & Set PIN

Grant Device Admin permission, set your parent PIN. KidPod becomes the default home screen.

3

Add Content via USB

Connect the phone to your computer, drag MP3/M4B files into /KidPod/Music/ and /KidPod/Audiobooks/.

4

Hand to your child!

KidPod scans the folders and shows all content. The phone is now a locked-down music player.
